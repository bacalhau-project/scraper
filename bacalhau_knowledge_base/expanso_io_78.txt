The Lab also welcomes its largest cohort to date with 17 multi-stage, global startups joining Batch #8 Fall Program
September 13, 2023 08:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time
BELLEVUE, Wash.–([BUSINESS WIRE](https://www.businesswire.com/))–[5G Open Innovation Lab](https://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&url=https%3A%2F%2F5goilab.com%2F&esheet=53553257&newsitemid=20230913122326&lan=en-US&anchor=5G+Open+Innovation+Lab&index=1&md5=f08d8a7c9e52c7c3b31dc54caccd9c8f) (5G OI Lab) announced today that international telecommunication leaders AT&T and Comcast have joined its innovation ecosystem. The Lab also announced today its Batch #8 cohort — its largest to date with over $313M in venture capital raised and 17 multi-stage startups embarking on 5G OI Lab’s Fall program, which begins this week.
